About Me

I obtained a M.S. degree in Electronic Science and Technology at Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Zhiguo Shi and Prof. Chaojie Gu. I obtained B.S. degree in Electronic Science and Technology from Zhejiang University in 2021.

I am interested in wireless sensing and networking systems, human-computer interaction and IoT. My current work focuses on Internet of Things, wireless communication and Low Power Wide Area Networks.
